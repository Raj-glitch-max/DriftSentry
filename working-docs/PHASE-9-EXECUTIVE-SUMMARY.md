## ğŸ’¡ PHASE 9 KEY INSIGHTS

### Insight 1: ICP > Everyone
```
Trying to sell to "anyone in DevOps" = 0% conversion
Selling to "VP Eng at 50-500 person SaaS spending $20K on AWS" = 10-20% conversion

Get specific about who needs you.
```

### Insight 2: Listen Before Pitching
```
30-second pitch about product = boring
5-minute listen about their pain, then 2-minute demo = engaged

80% of call should be them talking.
```

### Insight 3: Demo Their Problem, Not Your Features
```
"Our product has real-time detection" = meh
"You're losing $3K/month on forgotten resources, here's how we find it" = wow

Connect to money or pain. Always.
```

### Insight 4: Charge From Day 1
```
Free trial forever = no commitment = low usage = churn
14-day free â†’ $500/month = real commitment = they try hard = valuable feedback

Price filters for seriousness.
```

### Insight 5: Speed Wins
```
Slow company: Takes 2 weeks to onboard customer, still not set up
Fast company: Customer onboarded in 30 min, saw value same day

Velocity matters as much as product.
```
