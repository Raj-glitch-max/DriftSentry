## ‚úçÔ∏è WRITING TIPS

### Make Numbers Memorable
**‚ùå Wrong**: "We improved performance"
**‚úÖ Right**: "Reduced dashboard load from 4 seconds to 200ms (20x faster)"

### Be Specific
**‚ùå Wrong**: "We fixed the authentication bug"
**‚úÖ Right**: "JWT tokens weren't refreshing on expiry, causing users to get kicked out. We implemented a token refresh queue with exponential backoff."

### Use Examples
**‚ùå Wrong**: "Rate limiting prevents attacks"
**‚úÖ Right**: "Attacker tries 1000 login attempts in 10 minutes. Without rate limiting: maybe 100 succeed. With Tier 2 (5/min limit): blocked after 5 attempts."

### Show Your Work
**‚ùå Wrong**: "We optimized the database queries"

### Be Honest About Mistakes
**‚ùå Wrong**: "We nailed this from day 1"
**‚úÖ Right**: "We made 3 mistakes here and learned from each..."

## üì± FORMATTING FOR READABILITY

Result: 
- 101 queries ‚Üí 1 query
