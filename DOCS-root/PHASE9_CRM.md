## How to Fill This Out

### Finding Tier 1 Prospects:

**LinkedIn Search:**
```
Title: "VP Engineering" OR "CTO" OR "Head of DevOps"
Company Size: 50-500 employees
Industry: Software, SaaS
Location: United States
Keywords: AWS, Terraform, Infrastructure
```

**GitHub Search:**
```
Search for: users with Terraform repos
Filter by: Recent activity
Look for: Companies in their bio
Cross-reference: LinkedIn for title
```

**Twitter/X Search:**
```
Keywords: #DevOps, #AWS, infrastructure
Bio mentions: VP Eng, CTO
Tweets about: Cost optimization, drift
```

**Communities:**
- r/devops (active posters)
- AWS Slack groups (admins/power users)
- Terraform community (contributors)

## Status Definitions

- **Cold**: Not contacted yet
- **Emailed**: First email sent (track date)
- **Responded**: They replied (interested or not)
- **Demo Scheduled**: Call booked
- **Demo Completed**: Call happened
- **Customer**: Paid and onboarded
- **Churned**: Left/declined
